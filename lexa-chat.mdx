## What is Lexa Chat?

Lexa Chat is your AI assistant available at [lexa.chat](https://lexa.chat). It's a powerful platform that combines chat, AI tools, and file management to help you work smarter and more efficiently.

3. Sign in or sign up for an account
4. Start chatting with Lexa!
