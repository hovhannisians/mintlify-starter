## Google Drive Integration

Lexa Chat's Google Drive integration allows you to seamlessly access, analyze, and work with your Google Drive files directly within the chat interface. This powerful integration streamlines your workflow by connecting your cloud storage with AI-powered analysis and assistance.

## Understanding Google Drive Integration

### What Is Google Drive Integration?
- **Direct access**: Connect your Google Drive account to Lexa Chat
- **File management**: Browse, search, and access your Drive files
- **Content analysis**: Analyze documents, spreadsheets, and presentations
- **Collaborative workflow**: Work with shared files and team documents

### Benefits of Integration
- **Centralized access**: Manage all your files from one interface
- **AI-powered analysis**: Get insights from your Drive documents
- **Seamless workflow**: No need to download and upload files
- **Team collaboration**: Work with shared files and folders

## Setting Up Google Drive Integration

### Initial Setup
1. **Access integration settings**: Go to the integrations section
2. **Connect Google Drive**: Click "Connect Google Drive"
3. **Authorize access**: Grant necessary permissions to Lexa Chat
4. **Verify connection**: Confirm successful connection to your Drive

### Permissions and Security
- **Read access**: Lexa can read your Drive files for analysis
- **Selective sharing**: Choose which files and folders to share
- **Privacy protection**: Your data remains secure and private
- **Revoke access**: Disconnect integration at any time
